Two axes of protection

Leaves no trace

Cookies, cache, and web storage are not persisted between sessions and never sent externally. Browsing history stays on-device only and can be wiped with one tap.

Shows nothing

Designed for the moment a glance comes near, a brief hand-off, or a need to disguise. The screen auto-camouflages the instant you switch away.

Key features

  • Non-persistent storage for cookies, cache, and web storage
  • Browsing history stays on-device, never sent externally
  • Face ID-protected Private Mode keeps a separate space
  • Two-layer ad & tracker blocking (network + DOM)
  • Fingerprinting protection (Canvas, WebGL, AudioContext)
  • HTTPS enforcement and JavaScript dialog blocking
  • One-tap wipe by category (history, cookies, cache, more)
  • Focus Filter integration switches Private Mode automatically
  • Recently closed tab restore and reader mode
  • Video download and in-page search

About the name

"Umbric" combines Umbra (Latin for shadow) with the idea of a deft, well-considered move. Sit beside you without taking up space, slip out of sight the moment a glance comes near — your privacy, kept by your own hand.

Leaving no trace isn't about deceiving anyone. It's about taking care of your own privacy.

No trace. Nothing shown. — Umbric.
