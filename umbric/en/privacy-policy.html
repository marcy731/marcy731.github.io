Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

Information We Collect

Umbric does not send personal information to any server or perform external tracking. Cookies, cache, and web storage are cleared automatically when the session ends. Browsing history and bookmarks are stored on-device only and never sent externally. You can delete them at any time using "Clear History" in Settings or the one-tap wipe button in the address bar.

Data Transmission

Umbric does not transmit any user data to external servers. The app contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Ad Blocking

Umbric blocks ads and trackers at two layers: network-level (WKContentRuleList) and DOM-level (user scripts). Blocking can be enabled or disabled in Settings.

HTTPS Enforcement

All navigation is restricted to HTTPS. Access to HTTP sites is automatically blocked to ensure encrypted communication.

Camera & Microphone

Umbric denies all camera and microphone access requests from websites.

Camouflage Screen

When the app moves to the background or becomes inactive, a camouflage screen replaces the browser content to prevent exposure in the app switcher.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via the "Contact" option in the app's Settings screen.
